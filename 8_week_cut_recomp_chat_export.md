# 8-Week Cut + Recomp (Reference Format)

## 1) Body Metrics Snapshot
- **Body fat %:** 18–22% (visual estimate)
- **Fat mass:** ~18.5 kg
- **Lean mass:** ~65.5 kg
- **BMI:** 24.1 (requires height)
- **Notes:** Estimate only. Not medical advice.

## 2) Training Plan (6-day PPL Split)
**Summary:** High-frequency push/pull/legs with progressive overload and moderate volume.

**Weekly Split (repeat for 8 weeks):**
- **Day 1 – Push (strength):** Bench press 4x6-8 · Incline DB press 3x8-10 · OHP 3x6-8 · Triceps 3x10-12
- **Day 2 – Pull (strength):** Pull-ups 4x6-8 · Barbell row 3x6-8 · Face pulls 3x12-15 · Curls 3x10-12
- **Day 3 – Legs (strength):** Squat 4x5-8 · RDL 3x6-10 · Leg press 3x10-12 · Calves 3x12-15
- **Day 4 – Push (volume):** DB bench 3x8-12 · Lateral raise 3x12-15 · Dips 3x8-12 · Triceps 3x12-15
- **Day 5 – Pull (volume):** Lat pulldown 3x8-12 · Chest-supported row 3x10-12 · Rear delt fly 3x12-15 · Curls 3x10-12
- **Day 6 – Legs (volume):** Front squat 3x8-10 · Hip hinge 3x8-12 · Leg curl 3x10-12 · Lunges 2x12-16 steps
- **Day 7 – Rest / active recovery**

**Progression Rules:**
- Add 1–2 reps per set weekly until you hit the top of the rep range.
- Once all sets hit the top range, increase load 2–5%.
- Keep 1–2 reps in reserve on compounds; push accessories closer to failure.
- Deload every 4–6 weeks if performance stalls.

## 3) Nutrition Plan
**Daily calories + macros:**
- **Calories:** 2,150 kcal
- **Protein:** 170 g · **Carbs:** 210 g · **Fat:** 65 g

**Adjustment Rules:**
- If weight loss is <0.25 kg/week, reduce calories by 150–200 kcal.
- If weight loss is >1% body weight/week, add 150–200 kcal.
- Keep protein fixed; adjust carbs/fats first.

**Sample Day:**
- Breakfast: Greek yogurt + berries + oats (450 kcal)
- Lunch: Chicken salad bowl (550 kcal)
- Dinner: Salmon + rice + greens (650 kcal)
- Snack: Protein shake + fruit (300 kcal)

## 4) Notes + Next Steps
- Prioritize 7–9h sleep and 7–10k steps/day.
- Track weekly averages (weight + photos) before adjusting calories.
- Re-scan in 4–6 weeks to confirm trends.
