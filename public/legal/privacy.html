Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

MyBodyScan is operated by ADLR Labs (“MyBodyScan”, “we”, “us”, or “our”). This policy explains what information we collect, why we use it, how it is shared, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

  • Account and contact information: email address, display name, authentication provider, and support communications.
  • Scan media and wellness information: body photos, goals, preferences, equipment, dietary restrictions, workout and meal logs, and other information you choose to enter.
  • Results and generated content: scan estimates, visual observations, calculated values, plans, check-ins, food insights, and optional transformation-preview images.
  • Device and usage information: app version, device/browser type, IP-derived security information, crash and performance diagnostics, feature interactions, and push-notification tokens when you opt in.
  • Purchase information: products purchased, subscription status, credit balance, transaction identifiers, and limited billing metadata. Payment processors handle full payment-card details; MyBodyScan does not store full card numbers.

How we use information

  • Provide and personalize scans, visual observations, wellness plans, nutrition features, progress tracking, and support.
  • Process purchases, maintain credits and subscriptions, and prevent duplicate charges or credit use.
  • Secure, troubleshoot, measure, and improve the service, including fraud and abuse prevention.
  • Send service notices and, only when you opt in, progress or plateau notifications.
  • Comply with law and enforce our Terms of Service.

Scan media and AI processing

Photos and related inputs may be sent to contracted cloud and AI service providers solely to operate the requested scan or optional preview. Outputs are estimates and may be inaccurate. A transformation preview is a motivational illustration, not a prediction or guarantee of future appearance.

Sharing and service providers

We do not sell personal information. We disclose information only as needed to:

  • cloud hosting, authentication, storage, analytics, AI-processing, nutrition-data, customer-support, notification, and payment providers acting for us;
  • protect users, MyBodyScan, or others from fraud, abuse, or security threats;
  • comply with legal process or applicable law; or
  • complete a business transaction, subject to appropriate safeguards.

Retention and deletion

We retain information while your account is active and as reasonably needed to provide the service, maintain transaction and security records, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations. Account deletion is available in Settings and is designed to remove your authentication account, user data, and owned media, subject to limited records that must be retained by law or for legitimate fraud, billing, or security purposes. Export any history you want to keep before deleting your account.

Your choices and rights

  • Review or update available profile, plan, scan, meal, and workout information in the app.
  • Delete scans or your account using available Settings controls.
  • Control optional notifications in Settings and your browser or device settings.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or export where applicable.

Depending on where you live, privacy law may provide additional rights. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request.

Children and teens

MyBodyScan is not intended for children under 13. Users ages 13 through 17 may use the service only with permission from a parent or legal guardian.

Security and international processing

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No system is completely secure. Information may be processed in the United States and other countries where our providers operate.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy and will post the revised effective date. Questions or privacy requests: support@mybodyscanapp.com.
