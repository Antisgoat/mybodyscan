Privacy Policy

Last updated:

MyBodyScan (“we”, “us”, “our”) is operated by ADLR Labs LLC. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account & Contact: email, display name, and support communications.
  • Scan Media You Upload: photos/videos you choose to submit for body scan results.
  • Wellness Inputs: goals, preferences, equipment, dietary restrictions, and logs you enter (meals, workouts).
  • Technical: app analytics, device data, IP (for security & fraud prevention).
  • Payments: processed by our payment processor; we receive limited billing metadata (no full card details).

How we use information

  • Provide body scan results and personalized plans.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the app (analytics, troubleshooting).
  • Process purchases, manage credits/subscriptions, and provide support.
  • Send reminders and service notices you can control in settings.

Sharing

We use service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, payments) bound by contracts to process data on our behalf. We do not sell personal data.

Your choices

  • Access, edit, or delete uploads and logs in the app.
  • Delete your account & data in settings or by contacting support@mybodyscanapp.com.
  • Control notifications in settings.

Data retention

We retain data while your account is active. You may delete scans at any time. We remove account data upon verified deletion requests, subject to legal obligations.

Children & teens

MyBodyScan is for users 13+. Users 13–17 must have consent from a parent/guardian. Not for children under 13.

International (GDPR/UK GDPR)

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data and object/restrict certain processing. Contact us to exercise rights.

Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method is 100% secure; please use a strong, unique password.
